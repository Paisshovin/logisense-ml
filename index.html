How it works

From shipment data
to actionable insights

LogiSense processes your shipment data through our ML pipeline and delivers predictions your team can act on immediately.

Connect your data
Upload shipments via CSV or connect your TMS via our REST API. Works with any logistics platform.

ML scoring

Risk alerts
